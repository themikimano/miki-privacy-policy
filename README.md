# Privacy Policy

## Chrome Extensions

### 🎛️ Spotify Pause+
**Last updated:** April 9, 2026

**Spotify Pause+** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information of any kind.
What the extension accesses:

The extension monitors audio activity in your browser tabs (YouTube and Twitter/X) solely to trigger Spotify playback controls. This processing happens entirely on your device and nothing is sent anywhere.

**Third parties:**
No data is shared with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used.

**Changes:**
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted to this page with a new date.

**Contact:**
